WELCOME TO DALAGUETE

Swim in the milky-blue water of lagoons formed by springs and rent a cottage on the white-sand beach. Dalaguete is an affluent neighborhood with spectacular beaches and historic colonial landmarks. Explore the picturesque natural features surrounding the town. Relax on the stretch of white sand of Dalaguete Beach on the eastern coast. Its public beach is renowned for its cleanliness and amenities. Small private resorts feature swimming pools and panoramic views of the sea. Rent deck chairs and bungalows at the public beach to sunbathe in style.
